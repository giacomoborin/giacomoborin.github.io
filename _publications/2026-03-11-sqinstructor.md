## Abstract

We explore the use of level structures to generalize the SQIsign signature
scheme. We give a general framework where, given the public key and the
commitment, the challenge is to exhibit an isogeny between them with an
additional requirement, namely to map a chosen level structure to another. We
then instantiate the framework using 1-dimensional and 2-dimensional isogenies.
In doing that we provide a new explicit Deuring correspondence for supersingular
elliptic curves with level structures and solve new constrained norm equations.
