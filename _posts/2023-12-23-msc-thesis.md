RAW
======
I am studying Code-based post quantum signature schemes based on Cryptographic Group Actions and Code Equivalence assumptions; in particular LESS and MEDS with the supervision of [Alessio Meneghetti](https://webapps.unitn.it/du/it/Persona/PER0126300/Didattica), [Edoardo Persichetti](https://www.persichetti.me/home) and [Paolo Santini](https://www.dii.univpm.it/paolo.santini).
I am designing threshold versions of the signatures, overcoming the difficulty that the groups are non commutative. A part of the work will be <a href="https://giacomoborin.github.io/works/master/CBC23-LETSS.pdf">presented (🇬🇧)</a> at [CBCrypto2023](https://www.cb-crypto.org/program) and if successful the schemes will be submitted to the [NIST call for MPTC](https://csrc.nist.gov/Projects/threshold-cryptography).
Moreover I am also trying to find optimizations for LESS using code puncturing and to study the complexity of the underlying problems (for example in relation the TI-complete class).
